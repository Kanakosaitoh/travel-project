Why Croatia?

Beaches, beaches, beaches...

Croatia has 2500 miles of coastline and close to 1200 islands. Croatia has unpolluted marine areas with nature reserves and 116 Blue Flag beaches. Croatia was ranked first in Europe for swimming water quality in 2022 by European Environmental Agency.
